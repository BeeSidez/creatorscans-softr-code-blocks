Phase 1 of 5

Build Your Asset Library

Before any AI tool can create a convincing version of you, it needs raw material to work from. This is a one-time setup — do it properly once and you'll reuse these assets for every product you promote going forward.

The 5-phase system — you are here: Phase 1, Raw Materials.
1

The 20–30 Photo Rule

AI image generators need 20 to 30 clear photos of you from different angles to accurately recreate your face and body. You'll build two sets: a studio set and a lifestyle set.

Set A — Studio

15–20 photos against a plain white wall or green screen. Plain clothing (white tee, neutral trousers). Slight angles: straight on, left, right, up, down.

Set B — Lifestyle

10 natural photos around your home. Keep the clothing plain so the AI can swap outfits later. No filters, no sunglasses, no other people.

Why plain clothing? Because your twin will wear a different outfit in every ad — bathrobe, hoodie, gym kit — and plain reference clothes make outfit swapping incredibly clean.
2

Capture Your Background Plates

Your twin needs realistic places to "film" in. Instead of letting the AI hallucinate fake rooms, you'll photograph your actual home — this is what gives your ads that raw, authentic UGC feel.

The method: Stand in a corner of the room and take a photo with your phone's 0.5x wide-angle lens. Do this from all four corners. Make sure you are not in the frame.

Shoot the rooms that match your products: bathroom for beauty, kitchen for gadgets, bedroom or living room for lifestyle. The more rooms, the more variety you'll have later.

3

Record Four Reference Videos

Still photos teach the AI your face. Short videos teach it your movement — how your mouth shapes words, how your head tilts, how you naturally gesture. You need just four 2-minute clips.

Clip 1 & 2

Sitting down — one horizontal, one vertical. Speak directly to the camera as if you're reviewing a product.

Clip 3 & 4

Standing up — one horizontal, one vertical. Exaggerate your mouth slightly. The AI is learning how you talk.

It doesn't matter what you say — the AI is learning your movement patterns, not your words. Plain white background makes green-screening easy.
4

Clone Your Voice

Your twin needs to sound exactly like you. You have two routes depending on how much time you have and how realistic you need the result.

Speed Route

ElevenLabs instant clone. Record a 30–60s sample, upload it, and from then on you just type scripts. Fast and scales well.

Realism Route

Record the voiceover yourself, then feed the audio into HeyGen. Real breaths, pauses, inflections. The most convincing result.

Pro move: Combine both. Use manual voiceover for your hero ads and the ElevenLabs clone for rapid hook testing.
